How to Earn in Fortnite

Making earnings in Fortnite requires dedication. Focus on these key areas:

  • FNCS & Cash Cups: Consistently place high in official Epic tournaments. This is the primary way to earn prize money.

  • Wagers & Scrims: Participate in community-run wagers and pro scrims to sharpen your skills and win money.
